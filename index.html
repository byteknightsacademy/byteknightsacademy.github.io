FAQ's

What is Byte Knights?

▾

Byte Knights is a coding tutoring service that teaches kids programming concepts through engaging, project-based lessons in a fun, medieval-themed learning environment. Our goal is to help young learners become skilled coders, or "Byte Knights."

What Programming Languages Do We Teach?

▾

Our Head Knights are specialized in Python.

What Are Our Qualifications?

▾

Our tutors received a perfect score on the College Board AP Computer Science A Exam, are ranked academically in the top 3% of their high school class, scored 1500+ on the SAT, and have collectively taught 50+ students.

What Level Of Experience is Required To Start?

▾

No prior coding experience is required! Byte Knights is designed for both beginners and those with some foundational knowledge who want to enhance their skills.

How is the Curriculum Structured?

▾

Our lessons are module-based, with each module representing a specific coding concept that students must "overcome" to advance through the ranks of the Byte Knights. These lessons are project-based and designed to teach both fundamental coding concepts and develop problem-solving skills.

How Are Sessions Structured?

▾

Each session begins with 10-15 minutes dedicated to reviewing the previous week's topic. This is followed by a 15-20 minute quiz to assess understanding. Afterward, another 20-25 minutes are spent teaching the current lesson topic, and the remaining 30-45 minutes are focused on hands-on work, allowing students to apply the concepts they've learned.

How Do We Communicate?

▾

We keep parents and students informed through regular updates, including progress reports, project summaries, quiz results, and schedule changes or upcoming events via email and on our social media! For more detailed inquiries or feedback, parents can reach out directly to us through the email listed under the contact section of the website.

How Do I Pay?

▾

After registering your child/children through our website and completing the free trial session, further information regarding payments will be sent out to you via email.
